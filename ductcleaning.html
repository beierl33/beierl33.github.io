Duct Cleaning

Bosch Brothers Air Conditioning provides professional duct cleaning for homeowners in Punta Gorda, Port Charlotte, North Port, Cape Coral, and all surrounding areas. Clean ducts help keep your air fresh, improve airflow, and reduce allergens. This is especially important in Southwest Florida’s humid climate.

Comprehensive Ductwork Services

At Bosch Brothers Air Conditioning, we offer a full range of duct replacement services tailored to meet your specific needs. Our comprehensive services include:

  • Ductwork Inspection and Assessment: Our experienced technicians conduct a thorough inspection of your existing ductwork to identify any issues such as leaks, blockages, or damage.
  • Custom Duct Design and Replacement: We design and install new duct systems that are customized to fit the layout and requirements of your home, ensuring optimal airflow and efficiency.
  • Sealing and Insulation: Proper sealing and insulation of ductwork are essential for maintaining energy efficiency. We ensure your new ducts are sealed and insulated to prevent air leaks and energy loss.
  • Removal of Old Ductwork: We carefully remove and dispose of your old, inefficient ductwork, ensuring a clean and seamless transition to your new system.
  • Testing and Balancing: After installation, we test and balance the new duct system to ensure even airflow and consistent temperatures throughout your home.
  • Post-Installation Support: Our service doesn't end with installation. We provide ongoing support and maintenance to ensure your new duct system continues to perform at its best.
