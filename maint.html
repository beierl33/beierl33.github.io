  • Maintenance agreements are biannual preventative AC system plans. This will help improve your AC system's efficiency, extend your equipment's life, and restore cooling and heating capacity.

  • During a maintenance visit our technician will clean and check your condensing coil, test and adjust your system's operating pressure, test your system's starting capabilities, check freon levels, test the voltage of your motor, and much more.

  • Yes, we do. In addition to our AC system maintenance we also perform AC related services for the inside of your home. This includes calibrating your thermostat, measuring temperature differences, checking for proper airflow, and also changing any on-site filters.

  • Bosch Brothers only employees salaried, non-commissioned technicians. During a maintenance visit one of our professionally trained technicians will perform the preventative maintenance and look over your AC system. With Bosch Brothers you're given the guarantee of honest, reliable service; and employing non-commissioned technicians is what enables us to do just that.

  • Having a maintenance agreement with Bosch Brothers makes you a priority customer. Priority customers are given discounts on parts and labor, and given the reassurance knowing that they can call on Bosch Brothers to get same day service.
