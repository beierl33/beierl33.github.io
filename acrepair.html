AC Repair

When your AC stops cooling, Bosch Brothers Air Conditioning provides fast, reliable repair services for homeowners in Punta Gorda, Port Charlotte, North Port, Cape Coral, and all surrounding Charlotte County areas. Our experienced technicians diagnose issues quickly and restore comfort to your home—even in Southwest Florida’s extreme heat and humidity.

Comprehensive AC Repair Services

At Bosch Brothers Air Conditioning, we offer a wide range of AC repair services to address any issues your system may encounter. Our skilled technicians are equipped to handle:

  • Compressor Repairs: The compressor is the heart of your AC system. If it's not working properly, your entire system can fail. We diagnose and fix compressor issues efficiently.
  • Refrigerant Leaks: Low refrigerant levels can significantly reduce your AC's efficiency. Our experts will locate and repair leaks, then recharge your system to the optimal level.
  • Thermostat Issues: A malfunctioning thermostat can lead to uneven cooling or a complete system shutdown. We repair or replace faulty thermostats to restore your comfort.
  • Drainage Problems: Clogged condensate drains can cause water damage and reduce cooling efficiency. We clear blockages and repair drainage issues to prevent further problems.
  • Fan Motor Repairs: A broken fan motor can compromise your AC's ability to circulate air. We repair or replace damaged fan motors to restore proper airflow.
  • Electrical Problems: Faulty wiring or electrical components can cause your AC to malfunction. We identify and resolve electrical issues to ensure your system operates safely.
